Privacy Policy — Tabata Core

Effective date: 14 May 2026

This policy describes the Android app Tabata Core (package com.tabatacore.app).

What the app does

Tabata Core is a fullscreen gym timer (sets × reps with work and rest intervals). Optional sounds use bundled audio on your device. While a workout is running, the app may keep the screen awake so you can see the timer.

Data we collect

We do not operate user accounts for this app. Timer and display preferences (for example sets, reps, work/rest durations, warmup and cooldown options, sound, theme, colors, and dimming) are stored only on your device using the operating system’s local storage. We do not receive this information on our servers.

The app does not include advertising or analytics SDKs in its published design. Audio playback is local; the app does not upload recordings of you or your environment.

Third parties

Installing and updating the app is handled by Google Play and your device manufacturer under their own terms and policies.

Children

The app is a general-purpose fitness timer and is not directed at collecting personal information from children. Adjust this sentence to match your Play Console Target audience declaration if required.

Changes

We may update this policy. The effective date above will change when we do.
