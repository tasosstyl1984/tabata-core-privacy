Privacy Policy — Tabata Core

Effective date: 21 May 2026
Last updated: 9 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tabata Core (Android package com.tabatacore.app) handles information when you install or use the app from Google Play. It applies wherever the app is listed, including in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, and other countries.

1. Who we are

Tabata Core is published by the individual developer of the app (the “we”, “us”, or “developer”). We do not operate user accounts or our own servers for storing your workouts.

Contact (privacy and data requests): tasos.styl@gmail.com

2. Summary

Tabata Core is a local fullscreen timer for Tabata/HIIT workouts and optional reaction-style training. In the current version:

  • You are not required to create an account.
  • Your workout settings, templates, and session data are stored on your device unless you choose to share something using Android’s share feature.
  • We do not sell your personal information, show in-app advertising, or use analytics or crash-reporting SDKs that identify you.
  • We do not receive your workout data on our own servers.

3. Information processed on your device

The app processes the following categories of information locally to provide its features:

3.1 Workout and app settings

Saved using Android app storage (for example SharedPreferences), including:

  • Tabata structure: sets, reps, work/rest durations, optional warmup and cooldown
  • Saved Tabata templates (names and interval settings you keep)
  • Sound, vibration, theme, colours, screen dimming, and reaction-training options
  • Notification, wake-lock, and phone-call pause preferences
  • Optional StopWatch sync on/off preference

3.2 Active workout and resume

  • While a workout runs, the app tracks phase, timers, set/rep progress, and related state in memory and may write an optional resume checkpoint if the app is closed mid-session, until the workout ends or you clear app data.
  • After a workout, the app may show a workout summary (times, sets/reps, skips, restarts). The app keeps the most recent summary on your device so you can review it from the start screen until you finish another workout or clear app data.
  • The app may also save a workout history on your device (up to 300 ended sessions) with charts and past summaries. History is stored locally only and is removed if you uninstall the app or clear app data. You can export history as CSV or clear it from the history screen.

We do not receive your workout summaries or history on our servers.

3.3 StopWatch companion sync (optional)

If you enable StopWatch sync and connect a compatible Tabata Core StopWatch over Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), the app exchanges workout information directly with that device while a session runs, for example:

  • Timer state: phase, remaining time, set/rep index, pause, and optional manual cooldown
  • Optional motivation text shown during work intervals (when enabled in the app)
  • Interval sound on/off so phone and watch do not beep at the same time
  • Basic remote commands from the watch (for example pause, resume, stop, or skip)

This data is sent peer-to-peer over Bluetooth between your phone and watch. We do not operate StopWatch servers and do not receive this data. The watch may store its own local settings. BLE is used only for companion sync, not for location.

3.4 App version display

The app may read its own version and build number from the device (for example to show “v1.0.1” in Settings). This is used for display only and is not sent to us.

3.5 Share (optional)

If you tap Share on a workout summary, the app renders a PNG image into temporary app storage and opens Android’s system share sheet. You choose the recipient app (messages, email, cloud storage, etc.). We do not receive the image or summary. Any app you share with processes data under its own privacy policy.

4. Features that use device capabilities

While you use the app, it may use the following on-device capabilities. None of these upload your workout data to us:

  • Audio: bundled sounds for interval cues (no recording or upload of your voice or environment).
  • Vibration: optional haptic feedback on button taps when enabled in Settings.
  • Screen wake lock: keeps the screen on during an active workout so the timer stays visible.
  • Reaction mode (optional): changes screen colours during work intervals on a timer you configure; display only on your device.
  • Notifications (optional): an ongoing workout notification while a session runs in the background, showing phase and countdown generated on your device. On Android 13+, the system may ask you to allow notifications. On some Android versions the notification may appear as a live update. A foreground service may run while the notification is shown so timing stays reliable when the app is not in the foreground.
  • Phone-call pause (optional): if enabled, the app may pause when Android reports ringtone or in-call audio mode. The app uses audio mode only and does not read your phone number, call log, or contacts.
  • Screen dimming (optional): reduces brightness during Tabata workouts until you tap to brighten; preference stored locally.
  • Bluetooth / Nearby devices (optional): scan for and connect to a Tabata Core StopWatch for companion sync. Used only when you turn sync on; not used for location or advertising.

5. Android permissions

The app requests only permissions needed for the features above, for example:

  • Vibrate — optional button haptics
  • Wake lock — keep screen on during workouts
  • Post notifications (and, on supported devices, promoted notifications) — optional background workout notification
  • Foreground service — reliable workout notification while the app is in the background
  • Bluetooth scan and connect (Android 12+) — optional Tabata Core StopWatch companion sync; declared as not used for location

The app does not request access to your contacts, photo gallery, precise location, microphone, or camera for its core features.

6. What we do not collect

In the current version of Tabata Core we do not:

  • Ask for your name, email address, or phone number inside the app
  • Use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or identity-linked crash-reporting services
  • Operate cloud backup of your workouts on our servers
  • Sell or rent your personal information

Google Play or your device manufacturer may process limited technical or diagnostic data when you install or update apps; that processing is governed by their policies, not this document.

7. Legal basis (EEA / UK)

Where the GDPR or UK GDPR applies, we process on-device information only to provide and operate the app you request (timer, settings, optional notifications and related features). We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or direct marketing.

Our legal bases are typically performance of a contract (providing the app you download) and legitimate interests (operating and improving a secure local timer), balanced against your rights. Where processing depends on your choices (for example enabling notifications or Share), it is based on your consent through those system or in-app choices.

8. Retention

Information stored by the app remains on your device until you:

  • clear app storage or app cache in Android settings, or
  • uninstall the app.

Temporary files created for Share may remain in cache until the system clears them. We do not retain copies on our servers because we do not receive that data.

9. Your rights (EEA / UK)

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or object to processing of personal data, and to data portability, as well as the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Because we do not hold your workout data on our servers, most requests can be fulfilled by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage. For any other request, contact us at the email above. We will respond within the time required by applicable law (typically one month under GDPR).

If you are in the EU, you may also contact your local data protection authority. A list of EU authorities is available from the European Data Protection Board.

10. Children and age

On Google Play, Tabata Core is listed for users aged 13 and over (target age groups: 13–15, 16–17, and 18+). The app is a general fitness timer and is not directed at children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child under 13 has contacted us with personal data, email us and we will take appropriate steps.

Users aged 13–17 should use the app with a parent or guardian’s permission where required by local law.

11. International use

If you download the app outside your home country, your device and Google Play may still apply local settings. We do not operate a Tabata Core account system and do not receive your settings on our servers in the current version.

12. Purchases

Tabata Core is free to download on Google Play at the time of this policy. If a paid version or in-app purchase is offered in the future, Google will process payment. We will not receive your payment card or bank details. Purchase records are handled under Google’s privacy policy.

13. Security

We design the app to keep workout data on your device. No method of electronic storage is completely secure; you are responsible for securing your device (screen lock, updates, and careful use of Share).

14. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Material changes will be posted on this page and, where required, reflected in the Google Play store listing and Data safety information.

If we introduce features that collect or transmit personal data (for example cloud sync or analytics), we will update this policy before or when those features become available.
