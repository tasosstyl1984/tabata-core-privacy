Privacy Policy — Tabata Core

Effective date: 21 May 2026
Last updated: 2 June 2026

This policy describes the Android app Tabata Core (package com.tabatacore.app) when you download it from Google Play, including in the European Union and other countries or regions where the app is listed.

Summary

Tabata Core is a local workout timer for Tabata/HIIT and optional reaction training. We do not operate user accounts or backend servers for the app. Your timer settings and saved plans stay on your device; we do not receive them.

Who is responsible

The data controller for Tabata Core is the individual developer of the app (contact details below). For privacy questions or requests, email tasos.styl@gmail.com.

What the app does on your device

Tabata Core is a fullscreen gym timer (sets × reps with work and rest intervals). While you use the app, it may:

  • Store timer and display preferences locally (see below).
  • Save optional Tabata templates (named workout plans) on your device only.
  • Play bundled sounds for interval cues (no upload of audio from you).
  • Keep the screen awake during an active workout so the timer stays visible.
  • Use optional vibration on button taps when enabled in settings (local only).
  • Run optional Reaction mode during work intervals (screen colour changes on a timer you configure). This is display-only on your device; we do not receive it.
  • Show an optional ongoing workout notification while a session runs and the app is in the background, displaying phase and countdown on your device only. On Android 13+, you may be asked to allow notifications for this feature; we only show workout information generated on your device. On supported Android versions, the notification may appear as a live update—still local only, not sent to us.
  • Optionally pause the workout when the device reports an incoming ring or active call, using Android audio mode only. The app does not read your phone number, call log, or contacts.
  • After a session ends, show an optional workout summary on screen (time, sets/reps completed, skips and restarts). This summary is generated on your device and is not sent to us.
  • Optionally keep the most recent workout summary on your device so you can open it again from the start screen until you complete another workout or clear app data.
  • If you tap Share on that summary, the app creates a one-time PNG image in temporary app storage and opens Android’s system share sheet. You choose the destination (messages, email, cloud drive, etc.). We do not receive the image or summary; any app you share to processes it under its own privacy policy.
  • Optionally dim the workout screen while running in Tabata mode (display only; tap to brighten). This preference is stored locally.

Information stored on your device

We do not operate user accounts. Preferences are saved only on your device (Android app storage), for example:

  • Tabata structure (sets, reps, work/rest, warmup and cooldown)
  • Saved Tabata templates (names and interval settings you choose to keep)
  • Sound, vibration, theme, colours, dimming, and reaction-training options
  • Notification and call-pause settings
  • An optional workout resume checkpoint if the app is closed during an active session (phase and timers), until the workout ends or you clear app data
  • The optional last completed workout summary (for reopening from the start screen), until replaced or cleared

This information is used to run the app and restore your settings. We do not receive it on our servers. It remains on your device until you clear app storage or uninstall the app.

The app does not maintain a long-term workout history database. End-of-session summaries are shown after a workout; only the most recent summary may be kept locally for convenience (unless you share an image using Share, as described above). Temporary share images may remain in app cache until the system clears them.

Data we do not collect

In the current published app we do not:

  • Ask for your name, email, or phone number inside the app
  • Use advertising, analytics, or identity-linked crash-reporting SDKs
  • Access contacts, your photo gallery, precise location, microphone, or camera for app features (Share uses the system picker—you choose where data goes; we do not read your gallery)
  • Sell personal information

If a future update adds features that collect or transmit data (for example cloud backup), we will update this policy and Google Play’s Data safety section before or when those features go live.

Purchases

If you buy Tabata Core on Google Play, Google processes payment. We do not receive your card or bank details. Purchase records are handled under Google’s privacy policy.

Third parties

Installing, updating, and (where applicable) billing are handled by Google Play and your device manufacturer under their own terms and policies. We do not control OS-level diagnostic or usage data they may process.

Legal basis (EEA / UK)

Where GDPR or UK GDPR applies, on-device data is processed only to provide and operate the app (timer, settings, optional notifications you enable). We do not use it for advertising, profiling, or marketing.

Your rights (EEA / UK users)

You may have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or object to processing of personal data, and to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. Because we do not hold your workout data on our servers, erasure is achieved by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage in Android settings. Contact us at the email below for any request.

Age and target audience

On Google Play, Tabata Core is listed for users aged 13 and over (target age groups: 13–15, 16–17, and 18+). It is a general fitness timer and is not directed at children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone. The app does not require an account; workout preferences stay on the device only. If you are a parent or guardian and believe someone under 13 has used the app or contacted us with personal data, email us and we will take appropriate steps.

Users aged 13–17 should use the app with a parent or guardian’s permission where required by local law.

International use

If you download the app outside your home country, device and Play Store settings may still apply locally. We do not operate a Tabata Core account system or receive your settings on our servers.

Changes

We may update this policy. The dates at the top will change when we do. Material changes will be reflected on this page and, where required, in the Google Play listing.
